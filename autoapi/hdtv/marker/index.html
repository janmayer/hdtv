class hdtv.marker.Marker(xytype, p1, color=hdtv.color.zoom, cal=None, connecttop=False)

Bases: hdtv.drawable.Drawable

Markers in X or in Y direction

Every marker contains a pair of positions as many markers come always in pairs to mark a region (like background markers). Of course it is also possible to have markers that consist of a single marker, then the second position is None.
